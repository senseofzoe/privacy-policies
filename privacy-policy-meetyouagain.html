📌 Key point: This app does NOT collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. All information stays only on your device.

1. What Data We Do NOT Collect

We guarantee that the app does not request or transmit:

  • ❌ Name, email, phone number, or any other contact information
  • ❌ Location data
  • ❌ Heart rate, sleep, physical activity, or any other health metrics
  • ❌ Data from other devices or wearables
  • ❌ Viewing history, clicks, or behavioral analytics inside the app
  • ❌ Any data for ad networks or analytics platforms

The app works completely offline and does not use the internet to transmit data (except for Google Play license verification on first launch).

2. What Data Is Stored on Your Device

All information you create within the app is stored exclusively locally in your device's internal storage:

  • 📝 Texts and notes — letters, memories about your pet
  • 🖼️ Photos — images of your pet that you upload
  • 📅 Life dates — pet information (name, dates)
  • 🎨 Theme settings — your selected color scheme

We do NOT have access to this data. It is not copied, synced, or sent to our servers (because we don't have any servers).

3. Permissions

The app requests only one permission — and only when you choose to add a photo:

  • Photo Gallery Access (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE):
    Used exclusively to let you select a photo of your pet from your gallery. We do not scan other files and do not share images with third parties.

⚠️ This permission is requested only at the moment of photo selection and only with your explicit consent. You can revoke it at any time in Android Settings.

4. How to Delete Your Data

Since all data is stored only on your device, you can delete it in two ways:

  • Method 1: Uninstall the app from your device — this will permanently delete all notes, photos, and settings.
  • Method 2: Clear app data via Android Settings: Settings → Apps → [App Name] → Storage → Clear data.

Important: We cannot recover your data after deletion, as we do not store it ourselves. Please back up important photos to a separate folder on your device in advance.

6. Security

The security of your data depends entirely on the security of your device. We recommend:

  • Using screen lock (PIN, password, biometrics)
  • Enabling device encryption (if available)
  • Regularly updating your Android operating system

In case of loss or theft of your device, data can only be accessed with physical access to the device.
