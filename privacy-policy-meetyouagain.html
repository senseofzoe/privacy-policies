Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 23, 2026

Application: «Mail for CatStar»

Offline · No Ads · Free

Core Principle: We do not collect, store on servers, or share your personal data. All information you entrust to the application remains exclusively on your device.

1. General Provisions

This Privacy Policy (hereinafter — the "Policy") describes how the application «Mail for CatStar» (hereinafter — the "Application") processes and protects user information. By using the Application, you accept the terms of this Policy.

The Application is designed as a personal, safe space to preserve the cherished memory of your pet. We understand the sensitivity of your feelings and guarantee full confidentiality.

2. What Data We DO NOT Collect

We guarantee that the Application does not request, process, or transmit the following data:

  • First name, last name, email address, phone number, or any other contact information;
  • Location data (GPS);
  • Health data, sleep metrics, physical activity, or readings from wearable devices;
  • Browsing history, click patterns, or behavioral analytics within the Application;
  • Any device identifiers (IDFA, GAID, Android ID).

The Application operates entirely offline and does not use an internet connection to transmit user data. All Application operations occur locally on your device.

3. What Data Is Stored on Your Device

All information you create within the Application is stored exclusively locally in your smartphone's internal memory. This includes:

  • Texts and notes — letters, memories, and thoughts dedicated to your pet;
  • Photographs — images of your pet that you upload for your personal memorial diary;
  • Life dates — information about significant dates (birth, passing);
  • Application settings — your chosen color theme and display preferences.

Important: We have no technical ability to view, copy, or retrieve this data, as it never leaves your device and is not synchronized with any developer-operated cloud services.

4. Permissions

The Application requests access to device features only at the moment of your explicit action and solely to enable core functionality:

  • Photo Gallery Access (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE): requested only when you choose to add a photo of your pet. This permission is used solely to select an image from your gallery. We do not scan other files, analyze content, or transmit images to third parties.

You may revoke any permission at any time through your device's operating system settings. Revoking a permission may limit the corresponding feature but will not affect other Application functionalities.

5. Data Storage and Deletion

Since all data is stored only locally, you can permanently delete it in one of the following ways:

  • Uninstall the Application: deleting the Application from your device results in the irreversible removal of all notes, photos, and settings;
  • Clear data via Android Settings: Settings → Apps → «Mail for CatStar» → Storage → Clear Data.

Recovery of deleted information is impossible, as we do not maintain backups on our servers. We recommend saving important photos to a separate folder on your device or to a cloud service of your choice in advance.

6. Third-Party Services and Data Sharing

The Application does not use:

  • Analytics SDKs (including Firebase, AppsFlyer, Yandex Metrica);
  • Ad networks (Google AdMob, Yandex Ads, or any others);
  • Social plugins, "Share" buttons, or third-party website widgets;
  • Third-party cloud storage (iCloud, Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.).

The sole exception is Google Play Services, which is built into the Android operating system and is used exclusively for license verification (confirming the legitimate installation of the Application via the Google Play Store). Google's Privacy Policy is available here, and its terms are governed separately.

Sharing data with third parties is categorically excluded, as we do not collect data for transfer.

7. Security

The security of your data depends directly on the security of your device. We recommend:

  • Using screen lock (PIN, password, biometric authentication);
  • Enabling device encryption (if supported by your model);
  • Regularly updating your operating system to receive the latest security patches.

In the event of loss or theft of your device, access to data is only possible through physical access to it. We cannot remotely lock or delete data, as we do not manage it remotely.

8. Children

The Application is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect age-related information from users, as we do not require registration. Due to the absence of data collection, parental consent requirements do not apply to our Application.

We will notify you of material changes through Application updates in the store (in the new version description). The effective date is stated at the top of this document.
